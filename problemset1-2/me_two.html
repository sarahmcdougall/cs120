A Vacation Getaway

After I finished undergrad, I took a week-long trip up to Acadia National Park in Maine. The scenic views were well worth the 8-hour drive from Pennsylvania. I spent the week taking on a new hike each day, including Cadillac Mountain. Since that trip, I've ventured up to different parts of Maine when I have time (such as Kennebunkport and Portland). I can't wait to go back in the summer!

One of My Favorite Restaurants

One of my all-time favorite restaurants is Avocado Grill in West Palm Beach, Florida. Avocado Grill has an exciting atmosphere with live entertainment and beautiful outdoor seating. My favorite dish is the tuna poke bowl!

My Favorite Food

My all-time favorite food is ice cream. My favorite flavors are chocolate chip cookie dough and salted caramel! I also really enjoy pineapple soft-serve, although it is a lot more difficult to come by.


My Favorite Books

Name Cover Author Summary
Throne of Glass Sarah J. Maas This fantasy novel begins the Throne of Glass series, which follows assassin Celaena Sardothien as she competes in a deadly tournament with the hopes of freedom by the Crown Prince. The novel uncovers dark secrets both about Celaena and the kingdom.

I'll Give you the Sun Jandy Nelson "I'll Give you the Sun" paints the story of the complicated relationship between siblings who have undergone tragic loss and complex family and identity dynamics.
